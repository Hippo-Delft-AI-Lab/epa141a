# Actor Constellation

There are **30 actor groups** organised into **six negotiating blocs**.

```{figure} ../_static/justice_actor_clusters_map.png
:alt: JUSTICE actor clusters map
:width: 100%

Geographic distribution of the six negotiating blocs across the JUSTICE 57 world regions.
``` Each bloc deliberately mixes state delegations with non-state or institutional actors: industry lobbies, civil society organisations, financial institutions, and intergovernmental bodies. This means that even within your own bloc you will encounter conflicting interests and competing framings of fairness.
